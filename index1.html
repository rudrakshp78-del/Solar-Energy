2. Main Components Costs

SOLAR panels

  • Mono PERC panels: 18-25/watt
  • TOPCon panels: 22-30/watt
  • Bifacial panels: 25-35/watt

Inverter

  • 1-3 kW ₹15,000 - ₹40,000
  • 3-10 kW ₹40,000 - ₹1,00,000

Battery(optional)

  • Lead Acid Battery: ₹45,000 - ₹60,000 per kWh
  • Lithium-ion Battery: ₹60,000 - ₹80,000 per kWh

Installation

  • ₹5,000 - ₹15,000

3. Maintenance Costs

Annual maintenance:

  • Residential: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000/year
  • Commercial: ₹2,000 - ₹5,000/year

Cleaning:

  • Every 15-30 days
  • Dust can reduce output by 10-25%

4. Government Subsidy

Under India's rooftop solar schemes,eligible residential consumers can receive subsidy significantly reduce upfront costs.The exact amount changes over time and depens on system size and state

Examples

A 3 kW system:

  • Total COst:~₹1,5 lakh
  • After Subsidy:~₹80,000-₹1.1 lakh

Return on Investment(ROI)

FOR a typical home:

  • Electricity bill saving: ₹1,000-₹2,000/month
  • Payback period: 4-7 years
  • Panel lifespan: 15-25 years
  • Inverter lifespan: 10-15 years
